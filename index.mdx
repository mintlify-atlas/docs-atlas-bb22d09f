AI Engineering from Scratch is a comprehensive open-source course covering the complete AI engineering stack — from math foundations and classical ML through transformers, LLMs, and autonomous agent swarms. Every lesson produces a reusable artifact: a prompt, skill, agent, or MCP server you can use immediately.
